About the Magazine

We Like to
Look Forward

Put together by a family of writers, photographers and artists, tmrw is an independent lifestyle bible meets alternative encyclopedia, that packages music and culture in a manner that is both unique and radical. Driven by minimalism, design and the beautiful things in life, tmrw is all of your favourite things, as you’ve never seen them before.

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer worldwide shipping?

Yes, we deliver to every address in the world.

How do I find my nearest stockist?

Find your local stockist using the store locator below. Worldwide shipping is also available on single copies via our shop.

Why is each volume priced at £9.99?

tmrw is of the highest quality and has been described as ‘more of a book than a magazine’. Each tmrw volume consists of 230+ pages of art and culture, that is perfect bound and printed in full colour on fsc-approved uncoated paper in the united kingdom. We are proud to be the only publication that offers 20+ pages to the cover story and 10+ pages to each inside music feature.

Can I check the availability of stock on the shop?

Stock check is currently not available, however all volumes have a limited print run and are sold on a first come first served basis.

What makes tmrw different to other magazines?

tmrw is perfect for your coffee table and is a must-have lifestyle bible. We pride ourselves on quality and distancing ourselves from the perception of a ‘magazine’.

Do you offer a digital version of each volume?

No, the content inside each volume is exclusive to print, however our website offers a taste of what you can find in each print edition.

How do I pay on the shop?

You can pay securely on our web-store via Paypal or with a debit or credit card.

What currency do I pay in?

The store is priced in GBP (£) but will be converted to your local currency during the checkout process.

Can I subscribe?

You can subscribe to the next 6 months (3 print volumes) of tmrw or purchase each volume individually.

Who do I contact if I have a query about my order?

Please e-mail orders@tmrwmagazine.com

Can I make changes to an order?

You can set up an account when purchasing any product on the shop and this will give you the ability to make changes if required.

I need more information, where do I look?

Please e-mail info@tmrwmagazine.com for more info.
